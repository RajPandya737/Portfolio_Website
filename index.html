Anisync

Flask, SQLite, Beautiful Soup, API's, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Gunicorn, Nginx, Google Analytics, Digital Ocean

As an Osu! player myself, I always found it difficult to find maps to play when it was so tedious to search one by one.
